      "###How can I plot netcdf data using python?  \n",

      "2.  Use `ncview`.  [ncview](http://meteora.ucsd.edu/~pierce/ncview_home_page.html) is the quickest way to visually examine a netcdf file and while it wont give you publishable images, it is a great tool for initial analysis.\n",
